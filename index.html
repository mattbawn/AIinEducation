ChatGPT and Artificial Intelligence to Enhance Education

Online workshop to be held on Monday the 17th of July 2023 at 13:00

Background

Since ChatGPT launched at the end of 2022, there has been intense speculation and apprehension on the impact of artificial intelligence (AI) in higher education, particularly around issues of academic integrity. The successful application of AI tools to a wide array of knowledge synthesis tasks and the rapid speed of development in this area will undoubtedly secure their place as a feature in the research and industry landscapes.These landscapes call for proactive forward-looking policies and practices on how to best use these tools to sustainably enhance the student experience whilst preparing graduates for AI embedded futures.

Main Objectives

  • Provide a forum for experts in AI and education to come together and share best practice examples of the application of AI tools to improve the learner experience.
  • Invite industry experts and leaders to share their vision of future trends in development of AI technologies and how this may impact research and education.
  • Hear from employers on how these technologies will shape the workplaces and environments of future graduates.
  • Organise interactive discussion sessions, where participants will discuss fundamental questions about the pro and cons of the use of AGI in education.
  • Networking with other partners and institutions, we aim to coordinate educational actions and define new standards of learning while taking advantage of the new potential offered by AGI such as ChatGPT.
